About This Tool

            
What is a Prompt Helper?

            
A prompt helper is a tool designed to assist you in writing high-quality, detailed, and effective prompts for various Artificial Intelligence (AI) models. Instead of facing a blank canvas, a prompt helper gives you a structured framework to build your ideas, ensuring you provide the AI with enough information to generate the best possible results. This process is often called "prompt engineering."

            
How to Use This Tool (Step-by-Step)

            
    
                
  1. Select the AI Type: At the top, choose whether you want to create a prompt for a Text, Image, or Video AI.
    2. 
                
  2. Add Prompt Components: Click the "Add..." buttons to insert different building blocks for your prompt, like 'Subject', 'Style', or 'Task'.
    3. 
                
  3. Fill in the Details: Write your ideas in the input boxes of the cards that appear. Use the examples as a guide.
    4. 
                
  4. Reorder and Refine: Use the '✥' icon to drag and drop the cards to change the order of your prompt components.
    5. 
                
  5. Copy Your Prompt: Once you are satisfied with the live preview, click the "Copy Prompt" button and paste it into your favorite AI tool!
